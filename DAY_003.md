## APTITUDE (10)

1. A and B can do a piece of work in 12 days and 16 days respectively. They work together for 4 days, then B leaves. How long will A take to finish the remaining work?
2. A dishonest shopkeeper uses a weight of 800 gm instead of 1 kg and sells his goods at the cost price. Find his profit percentage.
3. A boat travels 24 km upstream in 6 hours and 20 km downstream in 4 hours. Find the speed of the boat in still water and the speed of the water current.
4. A, B, and C enter into a partnership with investments in the ratio 3:5:7. After a year, C’s share in the profit is ₹3,500 more than A’s share. What is the total profit?
5. A sum of money doubles itself at compound interest in 4 years. In how many years will it become 8 times itself?
6. Six bells commence tolling together and toll at intervals of 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, and 12 seconds respectively. In 30 minutes, how many times do they toll together?
7. A container contains 40 liters of milk. From this container, 4 liters of milk was taken out and replaced by water. This process was repeated two more times. How much milk is now contained in the container?
8. A batsman has a certain average of runs for 16 innings. In the 17th inning, he makes a score of 85 runs, thereby increasing his average by 3. What is his average after the 17th inning?
9. At what time between 4 and 5 o'clock will the hands of a watch point in opposite directions?
10. How many different words can be formed using all the letters of the word "BANANA"?

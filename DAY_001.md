APTITUDE (10)

1. If the ratio of A : B is 3 : 5 and B : C is 10 : 12, find A : C.
2. A train travels 300 km at 60 km/h and returns at 75 km/h. Find the average speed.
3. 40% of a number is equal to 2/3 of another number. Find the ratio of the two numbers.
4. A sum becomes 720 in 2 years at simple interest and 800 in 4 years. Find the principal.
5. The average of 5 numbers is 24. If one number is excluded, the average becomes 22. Find the excluded number.
6. A shopkeeper gains 20% after giving a discount of 10%. Find the ratio of marked price to cost price.
7. Two pipes can fill a tank in 20 minutes and 30 minutes. How long will they take together?
8. The ratio of present ages of A and B is 4 : 5. After 6 years, it becomes 5 : 6. Find the present age of A.
9. A man walks at 4 km/h and runs at 10 km/h. He walks for half the time and runs for half the distance. Find the average speed.
10. A number when divided by 7 leaves remainder 3. What is the remainder when its square is divided by 7?

## APTITUDE (10) 

1. Two taps can fill a tank in 15 and 20 minutes respectively. A third tap can empty it in 10 minutes. If all three are opened when the tank is empty, how long will it take to fill?
2. A shopkeeper marks an item 60% above its cost price. What maximum discount percentage can he offer during a sale to avoid any loss?
3. Two trains of equal length are running on parallel tracks in opposite directions at 54 km/hr and 36 km/hr. The faster train passes the slower one in 24 seconds. Find the length of each train.
4. The ratio of present ages of father and son is 7:3. After 10 years, the ratio becomes 2:1. Find the present age of the father.
5. The difference between compound interest (compounded annually) and simple interest on a sum for 2 years at 10% per annum is ₹124. Find the principal amount.
6. Find the smallest number which when divided by 12, 16, and 24 leaves a remainder of 7 in each case.
7. In what ratio must water be mixed with milk costing ₹30 per liter to gain 20% by selling the mixture at ₹25 per liter?
8. The average of 11 consecutive odd numbers is 49. Find the smallest number.
9. If March 5, 2024 was a Tuesday, what day of the week will it be on March 5, 2025?
10. A bag contains 6 red, 5 blue, and 4 green balls. Three balls are drawn at random. What is the probability that at least one is red?

## C PROGRAMMING SNIPPETS (10) 

1.
```c
int arr[] = {1, 2, 3, 4, 5};
int *p = arr;
printf("%d %d", *p++, *(p++));
```

2.
```c
int x = 10, y = 5, z = 3;
z = x++ - --y + ++z;
printf("%d %d %d", x, y, z);
```

3.
```c
int a = 10, b = 20, c = 30;
int *ptr1 = &a, *ptr2 = &b;

printf("%d %d %d", a, b, *ptr2);
```

4.
```c

printf("%u ", num >> 4);
printf("%u", num | (num << 4));
```

5.
```c

```

6.
```c

while(*s) {

```

7.


8.
